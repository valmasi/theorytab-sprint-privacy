Privacy Policy
Effective date: 27 June 2026
TheoryTab Sprint: UK Theory Test is designed to work locally in Chrome.
Data collected
The extension does not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal data.
Data stored locally
The extension stores the following data locally in Chrome storage:
Theory test date
Study mode
Daily target
Practice progress
Question history
Weak-area statistics
Streak count
Notification preference
Stripe checkout URL configured by the publisher
This data remains on the user's device unless the publisher later adds a separate account, analytics, or sync service.
Remote services
Version 1.0.0 does not use remote code, analytics, advertising, or tracking.
Notifications
If enabled, the extension uses Chrome notifications to show practice reminders while Chrome is open. Users can turn reminders off in settings.
Payments
The extension opens a Stripe Payment Link for the optional GBP 2.99 30-Day Pass Pack. Stripe processes payment information. TheoryTab Sprint does not receive, store, or process payment card details.
Stripe may collect payment and billing information according to Stripe's own privacy policy and terms.
